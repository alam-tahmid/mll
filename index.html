About Us

Founded in 1992, MGH Logistics Ltd. has evolved to become an end to end supply chain service provider with over 40+ offices in 16 countries, including 1000+ employees worldwide. We offer you our expertise in identifying the fundamental needs of the customers, and in delivering sustainable solutions with visibility, integration, and flexibility. ​ We design effective faster and cheaper solutions for our customers whom we consider as our partners. We constantly evaluate the current practices to identify scopes for further improvement, and offer flexibility to enact necessary adjustments to stay ahead of time. Our round the clock tracking and PO management solution at www.myshipment.com allows our clients to access, manage, and track their commodity from anywhere. We have a diversified client base including globally renowned Fashion Retailers, Hypermarkets, FMCG, and Automotive industry. With a focus on personalized service, competitive rates, and customer satisfaction, we’re always striving to meet and exceed our high standards and our clients’ expectations.
